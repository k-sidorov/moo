Monday's Outstanding Orations

Focused, technical, weekly seminar on combinatorial optimization and related topics at TU Delft.

See the upcoming sessions I want to give a talk

We speak model-and-solve

Are you working with constraint satisfaction, SAT solving, mixed-integer programming, or any other related framework? We discuss these topics regularly, and many of us are actively involved in a relevant research.

Focused discussions

We do not segregate the presentation time and audience time: attendees are free to ask questions at any time. Sounds interesting? Then prepare about 20 minutes of the content, and we will support the discussion for the rest of the meeting!
